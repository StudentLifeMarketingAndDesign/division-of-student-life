Find a Performing Arts Organization

UI students enjoy a wealth of performance resources on and near campus, including world-renowned faculty members and impressive venues.

Find Opportunities on Campus

The Division of Performing Arts, which is part of the UI College of Liberal Arts and Sciences, includes Art Share, the Department of Dance, the School of Music, and the Department of Theatre Arts.

Student Organizations - Student Organizations include Intersection, the Juggalos, and the Latin American Dance Club.

Muscial Opportunities - From new music to jazz, and from symphonic classics to opera, there are at least two dozen groups for which musically inclined students may audition.

Dance Opportunities - Gain dance experience in areas ranging from modern to ballet to jazz and funk on stage at Iowa.

Theatrical Opportunities - UI undergraduates become involved quickly in classes and productions. All students are invited to audition for every play.

Arts Share - Arts Share is an outreach program in which UI faculty and student artists conduct interactive performances, workshops, readings, residencies, and masters classes across the state of Iowa.
