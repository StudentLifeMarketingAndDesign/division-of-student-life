From student groups in your major department to honor societies that recognize academic accomplishments, start your studies at Iowa off right by joining an academic organization.

Academic Student Organizations

There is a broad range of organizations for UI students who want to begin building their academic involvements outside of the classroom. These academic organizations include groups like the Art History Society, the award winning Mock Trial Club, and the Theta Tau Professional Engineering Fraternity.

University of Iowa Honors Program

Students in the University of Iowa Honors Program have a variety of activities from which to choose. Sponsored programs encompass honors initiatives for art, discourse, diversity, foreign relations, and volunteering. All help carry out Iowa's commitment to learning by doing.
